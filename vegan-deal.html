Vegan Deal

Vegan Deal is an application that support newly vegans to start off their journey for a change to a healthier lifestyle.

The goal of our application is to give tips for a healthy daily menu and lifestile. Creating a community that supports you is one of our goals.

I chose this project because as an ex vegan that would like to go back to that lifestyle and I recognize that there is a lack of guidence for who chose to follow this path.
