About me

I’m a UX/UI Designer with a degree in Communication, Languages, and Cultures who is interested in inspiring through my work and making meaningful connections with beautiful people. ​While transitioning into UX, I found my former career and experiences useful. It has developed my interpersonal skills and I am used to taking an empathetic approach to solving problems. I am caring, passionate, and curious about creating great experiences. This is why I feel UX Design is an amazing fit for me. I'm Colombian, I grew up in Italy and love brought me to Germany. This helped me to cherish diversity in all its forms and showed me the importance of bringing diverse prospectives to the table. My key skills include: Discovery: Research your users empathise with them, competitor analysis, conduct interviews, and gain insights Synthesizing: Take what you learn from your research Wireframing: Begin initial designs on your screens. Hierarchy: Learn how to visually prioritise your screen content and features. Usability Testing: Test your designs in front of real users. Proficient design tools such as Figma, InVision, Balsamiq, Miro
